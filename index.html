The 7 Major Chakras

  1. Root Chakra (Muladhara):

    • Location: The base of the spine (tailbone).

  2. Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana):

    • Location: Below the navel (belly button) (Stelter).
    • Body Function:
      • Sacrum: The sacrum forms the posterior pelvic wall and strengthens and stabilizes the pelvis (C.).
      • Pelvis: primary function of the pelvic bones is to transfer the load of the upper body onto the lower limbs while standing or walking (Burgess and Lui).
